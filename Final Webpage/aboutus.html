ABOUT

The NJIT art club was formed during the spring semester of 2017. Our club's goal is to bring art into the STEM field. We want to create a community for NJIT students to express themselves artistically. We also want to spread the love for art to our community outisde of NJIT by reaching out to areas in the Newark area. Art can also be used as a way of relieving stress and can create avenues for members to explore new fields and mediums of art.
